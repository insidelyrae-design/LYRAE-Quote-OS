## What It Does

Quote OS has one job: *Generate postable quotes for your brand.*

### Core Features

*1. Quote Generator* 
- 100+ pre-written luxury quotes across 5 niches: business, wellness, beauty, mindset, luxury
- Vibe modifiers: Soft, Bridge, Baddie, Ruthless
- Outputs branded with your name + sign-off
- No LYRAE branding in output

*2. Image Prompt Builder* 
- Converts any quote into Midjourney/Sora prompt
- 4 styles: Soft, Bridge, Baddie, Ruthless
- 4 layouts: Portrait, Collage, Lifestyle, Cover
- 2 ratios: 9:16 Reels, 4:5 Feed
- 8 content presets: Soft Morning, Becoming Her, Ruthless Authority, etc.

*3. Caption Library*
- 5 caption modes: Soft, Cold, Luxury, Dangerous, Becoming Her
- Auto-generates from your quote
- Copy/paste ready

*4. Brand Installation*
- Set your brand name once
- Set your sign-off line
- Saved in browser. No login. No database.
- All outputs use YOUR brand
